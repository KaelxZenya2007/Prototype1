# Lords Mobile Tools

An Automation Tools for Lords Mobile to lessen your worries!

## Features

### 🚀 Speed Up Calculator
Calculate the total speed up from your bags.

### ⚙️ Gear Simulation
Simulate different gear combinations and configurations to find the perfect loadout for your heroes. Test various strategies before forging any gear.

### 👥 Troops Calculator
Plan your army composition with precision. Calculate troop counts, training costs, and resource requirements for your strategic deployments.

### 💎 Gem Prediction
Predict gem outcomes and plan your gem investments wisely. Maximize your chances of getting the perfect gems for your bargain store spending.

### 🏛️ Army Colosseum Rotation
Stay updated with the current Army Colosseum rotation schedule. Plan your formations and strategies based on the active rotation.

## How to Use

1. Visit the application
2. Select the tool you want to use from the menu
3. Input your game data and preferences
4. Get instant calculations and recommendations
5. Optimize your gameplay strategy!
